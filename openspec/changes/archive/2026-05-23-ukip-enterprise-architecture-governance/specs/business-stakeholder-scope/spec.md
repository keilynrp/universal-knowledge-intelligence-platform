## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: UKIP maps capabilities to stakeholder outcomes
UKIP SHALL connect strategic capabilities to stakeholder segments, decision contexts, and measurable outcomes.

#### Scenario: Executive dashboard capability is proposed
- **WHEN** a dashboard feature is proposed for research executives
- **THEN** it identifies stakeholder decision context, expected outcome, evidence requirements, and success metrics

#### Scenario: Data steward workflow is proposed
- **WHEN** a mapping or reconciliation review workflow is proposed
- **THEN** it identifies the data steward task, review burden, data quality impact, and downstream stakeholder value

### Requirement: Stakeholder scope informs architecture priority
UKIP SHALL use stakeholder scope and business capability impact to prioritize strategic architecture work.

#### Scenario: Two specs compete for implementation priority
- **WHEN** multiple strategic specs are ready for implementation
- **THEN** UKIP evaluates stakeholder value, dependency criticality, risk reduction, and architectural leverage
