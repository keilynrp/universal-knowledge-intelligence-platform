## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: UKIP visual language balances freshness with scientific trust
UKIP SHALL express a fresh, distinctive, evidence-based visual language without sacrificing institutional credibility.

#### Scenario: Dashboard surface is designed
- **WHEN** a dashboard surface presents strategic intelligence
- **THEN** the design uses clear hierarchy, restrained surfaces, meaningful color, and narrative sequencing
- **AND** avoids decorative effects that do not support interpretation

#### Scenario: Brand color is used
- **WHEN** violet is used as a primary visual accent
- **THEN** the usage supports brand recognition, primary actions, or important narrative emphasis
- **AND** does not make every state appear equally important

#### Scenario: Intelligence surfaces use calm density
- **WHEN** analytic screens display rich information
- **THEN** they are organized for scanning, comparison, and repeated use
- **AND** avoid unnecessary framed containers or nested decorative cards

#### Scenario: Executive surfaces balance trust and engagement
- **WHEN** executive-facing surfaces (reports, dashboards, decision readouts) are designed
- **THEN** they feel modern and trustworthy
- **AND** prioritize evidence clarity over visual novelty

### Requirement: Product inspiration is translated, not copied
External design inspiration SHALL be translated into UKIP-specific scientific intelligence patterns.

#### Scenario: Nubank-inspired freshness is adopted
- **WHEN** UKIP adopts a lively violet-forward visual direction
- **THEN** it adapts that energy to research intelligence, evidence trust, and stakeholder decision support
- **AND** avoids copying fintech-specific brand expression

#### Scenario: Visual direction serves analytic product needs
- **WHEN** visual design decisions are made
- **THEN** they prioritize data readability, provenance clarity, and stakeholder trust
- **AND** do not sacrifice analytic density for decorative treatment

### Requirement: UX surfaces align with design system principles
UKIP SHALL align key product surfaces with design system principles for narrative metrics, evidence traceability, and responsive behavior.

#### Scenario: Dashboard widgets support narrative metrics
- **WHEN** dashboard widgets are designed or refactored
- **THEN** they tell a decision story: current state, readiness, evidence gaps, next action, and confidence limits
- **AND** KPI widgets are narratively useful, not merely descriptive

#### Scenario: Entity detail aligns with provenance layering
- **WHEN** entity detail surfaces are rendered
- **THEN** they show original ingestion, canonical identity, enrichment observations, authority/audit data, null reasons, and confidence/review status
- **AND** each section uses governed visual treatments

#### Scenario: Import and mapping flows are reviewable
- **WHEN** import/mapping UI renders source profiling and mapping suggestions
- **THEN** the UI makes suggestions reviewable with clear confidence, evidence, and action affordances

#### Scenario: Reports preserve executive readability with evidence
- **WHEN** reports are generated for stakeholder consumption
- **THEN** they are readable at executive level
- **AND** expose evidence and confidence through expandable or linked detail

#### Scenario: Pilot mode surfaces are touch-friendly
- **WHEN** pilot mode controls and surfaces are rendered
- **THEN** they use clear, calm, touch-friendly controls with adequate spacing
